Dairy Farm Ledger

The Dairy Farm Ledger provides a comprehensive system for managing dairy farm operations efficiently. It maintains Customer Milk Records, allowing farmers to track the daily supply of milk, including quantity, fat content, and rate per liter. To support farm operations, the system facilitates Cow Feed Orders, enabling farmers to purchase feed directly while recording the associated quantities and costs. Additionally, an Automatic Payment Deduction feature ensures seamless transactions by deducting the cost of ordered cow feed from the farmerâ€™s milk earnings. The Rate Management system allows for setting dynamic milk rates based on fat content, quality, or seasonal variations. Farmers can generate Payment Reports on a weekly or monthly basis to review their total milk supply, payments received, and deductions made. Furthermore, the system includes Balance Management, keeping a record of pending balances if deductions exceed earnings, ensuring transparency and financial stability for all stakeholders.
